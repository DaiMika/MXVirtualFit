About MXVirtualFit

MXVirtualFit was inspired by the need for accessible and effective fitness solutions in the digital age. We believe everyone should have the tools to achieve their fitness goals, regardless of their location or schedule. This project began as a personal quest for better health and evolved into a comprehensive platform to assist others on their fitness journeys.

This is a Portfolio Project for Holberton School.
